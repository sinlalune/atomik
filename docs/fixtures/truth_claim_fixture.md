# Wikipedia is maintained through collaborative editing

Wikipedia's articles are created and revised through collaborative editing rather than by one central author.

> **Truth status:** factual · local source-backed · source-linked · stable-domain  
> **Workflow status:** human-accepted — this records editorial acceptance, not proof by itself.

## Scope

This claim describes the general editing model. It does not imply that every article has equal review quality, editor diversity, stability, or subject expertise.

## Counterclaims or qualifications

- Some pages have protection, editorial policies, bots, administrators, and differing participation patterns.
- “Collaborative” does not mean every statement reflects universal consensus.
